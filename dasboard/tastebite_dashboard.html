Key Loyalty Insights
Loyalty members are 2.4× more likely to be highly satisfied (19.2% vs 8.1%) — the programme is delivering measurable value.
Only 201 customers are both loyalty members AND highly satisfied — TasteBite's most valuable core segment and the primary focus for retention investment.
Digitally engaged customers show the highest satisfaction across the board — growing digital adoption (reservations, delivery, loyalty enrolment) is a compounding retention lever.
52% of customers remain unenrolled in the loyalty programme — a significant acquisition opportunity, especially among Indian and American cuisine preferences.
